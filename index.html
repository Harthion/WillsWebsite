Photography

Ethereal Landscapes

A collection of moody landscape photographs capturing the raw beauty of nature through dramatic lighting and atmospheric conditions. Each image tells a story of solitude and wonder.

Technical Details

Camera: Sony A7R IV
Lenses: 16-35mm f/2.8, 70-200mm f/2.8
Editing: Adobe Lightroom, Photoshop

Artistic Intent: To showcase the emotional depth of natural landscapes and inspire viewers to connect with the environment around them.

Urban Portraits

Intimate portrait series exploring human connection in urban environments. Each subject is captured with natural light, emphasizing authenticity and emotional depth.

Technical Details

Camera: Canon EOS R5
Lenses: 85mm f/1.4, 50mm f/1.2
Editing: Capture One, Adobe Lightroom

Artistic Intent: To capture the vulnerability and strength of individuals within the urban landscape, celebrating diversity and human resilience.

Street Chronicles

Documentary-style street photography capturing candid moments of city life. These images showcase the rhythm and energy of urban environments.

Technical Details

Camera: Fujifilm X-T4
Lenses: 23mm f/1.4, 35mm f/1.4
Editing: Adobe Lightroom, VSCO

Artistic Intent: To document the unguarded moments that define urban culture and showcase the beauty in everyday interactions.

Cinematography

"Midnight Frequency" - Short Film

A noir-inspired short film exploring themes of isolation and connection in the digital age. Shot entirely at night using practical lighting to create an atmospheric, cinematic experience.

Technical Details

Camera: RED Komodo 6K
Lenses: Sigma Cine Primes 18-50mm
Editing: DaVinci Resolve, After Effects

Artistic Intent: To create a visual metaphor for modern loneliness while celebrating the power of human connection through cinematic storytelling.

High-energy music video featuring dynamic camera movements and vibrant color grading. The visual style complements the electronic music with futuristic aesthetics.
